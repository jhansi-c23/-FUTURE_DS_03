# 🛍 Online Retail Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes online retail transaction data to evaluate:

- Revenue performance
- Sales quantity trends
- Country-wise demand
- Top customers by revenue
- Monthly revenue patterns
- Funnel-style performance insights

The dashboard provides business-driven insights to support strategic decisions in marketing, sales, and operations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

This analysis answers key business questions:

- What is the total revenue performance?
- Which countries contribute the most revenue?
- Who are the top revenue-generating customers?
- How does revenue trend month-over-month?
- Where should the business focus for growth?

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Monthly Revenue Trend – 2011
- Shows revenue performance month-wise
- Identifies seasonal spikes and low-performing months
- Highest month observed: ~113K revenue

### 2️⃣ Top 10 Countries by Revenue & Quantity (Excluding UK)
- Highlights international market performance
- Country contributing highest revenue: 8.2M
- Helps identify strong expansion markets

### 3️⃣ Product Demand by Country (Geo Map)
- Visualizes geographic distribution of sales
- Helps in regional marketing strategy planning

### 4️⃣ Top 10 Customers by Revenue
- Highest revenue customer: 1.45M
- Enables VIP customer targeting
- Supports loyalty & retention strategy

### 5️⃣ Filters Available
- Country filter
- Month filter

These allow dynamic performance exploration.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- A small number of customers contribute a large portion of revenue (Pareto effect).
- International markets (excluding UK) contribute significantly to overall revenue.
- Revenue shows noticeable monthly fluctuation indicating seasonality.
- Certain countries generate high revenue with lower quantity, suggesting higher unit prices.
